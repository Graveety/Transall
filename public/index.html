About Company

Conceived as purely indigenous in nature

Transall is highly focused on raising the present level of local construction and maintenance expertise in Nigeria to internationally acceptable height. High premium is thus placed on recruiting the best hands in the construction industry worldwide .

We have, therefore, assembled a sound team of highly qualified, seasoned and committed key personnel and that is not all. There is also an array of state-of-the-art plant and equipment suitable for the general needs of the company.
